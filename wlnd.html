Day-1

Breakfast

2 boiled eggs with whole wheat toast or chapati

Lunch

Grilled chicken breast with mixed vegetable curry and a small portion of brown rice

Snacks

A cup of green tea with a slice of grilled chicken or fish

Dinner

Fish curry with a side of sautéed spinach or mixed greens

Day-2

Breakfast

Vegetable omelette made with 2 eggs and lots of vegetables

Lunch

Grilled fish with mixed vegetable salad and a small portion of quinoa or whole wheat chapati

Snacks

A cup of yogurt with a sprinkle of flaxseeds

Dinner

Chicken tikka with mint chutney and a side of roasted vegetables

Day-3

Breakfast

Scrambled eggs with onions, tomatoes, and spices, served with whole wheat toast

Lunch

Chicken curry with a side of steamed brown rice or millet (bajra)

Snacks

A handful of roasted chickpeas (chana)

Dinner

Grilled fish or chicken with a side of stir-fried vegetables

Day-4

Breakfast

Moong dal cheela (pancake) with mint chutney

Lunch

Mutton curry with a small portion of whole wheat chapati or brown rice

Snacks

A cup of buttermilk or a small cup of lassi

Dinner

Tandoori chicken with cucumber raita and a side of roasted vegetables

Day-5

Breakfast

Upma made with semolina and mixed vegetables

Lunch

Fish curry with a small portion of whole wheat chapati or quinoa

Snacks

• A cup of green tea with a small slice of grilled chicken or fish

Dinner

Chicken biryani made with brown rice and served with cucumber raita

Day-6

Breakfast

Vegetable poha (flattened rice) with peanuts and spices

Lunch

Grilled chicken or fish with mixed vegetable salad and a small portion of millet (jowar)

Snacks

A small bowl of low-fat yogurt with sliced fruits

Dinner

Egg curry with a side of steamed vegetables or salad

Day-7

Breakfast

Masala dosa with sambar and coconut chutney

Lunch

Chicken or fish curry with a small portion of brown rice or whole wheat chapati

Snacks

A cup of green tea with a small slice of grilled chicken or fish

Dinner

Vegetable stew with boiled egg or grilled chicken, served with a small portion of whole wheat bread or appam.
